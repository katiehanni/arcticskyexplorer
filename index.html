Dataset

NASA MODIS (MODerate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer)

Daily global satellite imagery with multi-spectral bands (Band 1: Red, Band 2: NIR, Band 3: Blue) for color composite visualization. 20+ years of temporal coverage accessible via NASA Worldview/GIBS API.
